Mr.Levinson

Mr.Levinson is a World History teacher at WWP High School South and was South Debate Club’s previous advisor. He served as the advisor for over 10 years and helped the club grow in immeasurable ways. Under his guidance, South received more national and regional awards than at any other point in its history. He also helped introduce multiple new competing formats including Public Forum, now South’s main debate format, and Big Questions, South’s fundraising tournament. Mr.Levinson was an integral part of the team and his guidance and mentoring will truly be missed.
