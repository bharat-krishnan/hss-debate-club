Debate Bible

Overview

The Debate Bible is an independent student project created by a South Debate alumni that strives to shed light on many of the nuances and strategies of debate that can only be learned through practice. It can be used as a guide on what to do and what not to do at competitions. It is still currently in progress so the guide only reviews the CVC format at this time.
