Who we are?

South Debate Club is West Windsor Plainsboro High School South’s official debate team. We are active members of the NJSDL and National Circuit, with many of our members qualifying for the National Tournament of Champions. Our primary formats for debate are Policy, Public Forum, and Moot Court, and we have seen considerable amounts of success in all 3 formats. We have meetings every Tuesday from 3-4 pm where we teach public speaking fundamentals, analyze topics for upcoming tournaments, and have fun! We’re a tight knit community that cares a lot about debate and about each other, but we are always looking for new members. If you are at all interested, we encourage you to sign up for our email list or come to any of our weekly meetings to learn more!
